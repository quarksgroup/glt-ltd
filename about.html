Global Link Travels Limited. (GLT) was established in 2017 as a licensed consultancy organization. It has ever since done its operations with qualified professionals with more experience in both medical tourism and travel industry.

GLT is a certified healthcare support agency with a versatile medical approach using up-to-date facilities and standards for easy access to specialized healthcare solutions at lucrative rates.

All GLT departments are under one roof. Ie. Management, Administration, Operations, Reservations, Sales & Marketing, Inbound & Outbound flight jets, as well as ticketing affairs. Hence a competitive advantage of all being under one roof.
